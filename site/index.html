This is the personal site of Nicholas Ham. I hold a doctorate in mathematics from the University of Tasmania, which I did under the supervision of Des FitzGerald and Peter Jarvis.

On top of completing a pure mathematics major during undergrad, I also completed majors in computer science and economics. Furthermore I contributed to designing and building an international programming competition sponsored by Google, and have experience teaching both mathematics and economics classes at the undergraduate level.
